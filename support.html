Account and Login Help

Printy 3D supports account access through available sign-in methods in the app, including supported third-party authentication providers such as Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In where available.

If you are unable to access your account, please contact support and include the email address connected to your account.

Listings and Product Content

If you experience an issue while creating, editing, or viewing a listing, please contact us with a short description of the issue and, if possible, screenshots or the product/listing details.

Users are responsible for ensuring that their listings, images, descriptions, and other uploaded content comply with our Terms of Service and applicable laws.

Reporting Content or Users

If you believe that content or conduct on Printy 3D violates our Terms of Service, intellectual property rights, or applicable law, please use the in-app reporting tools when available.

You may also contact us at printy31825@gmail.com with relevant details so we can review the report.

Account Deletion
