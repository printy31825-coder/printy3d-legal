Delete Your Printy 3D Account

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This page explains how users of Printy 3D, operated by Printy 3D, can request deletion of their account and associated data.

You can delete your account directly inside the app, or request deletion by email if you no longer have access to the app.

Before you delete your account

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, store, listings, uploaded images, saved data, notification tokens, and related account information where possible.

This action cannot be undone.

Printy 3D does not currently offer subscriptions or in-app purchases, so no billing cancellation is required before deleting your account.

Option A: Delete your account in the app

If you still have Printy 3D installed and can sign in, this is the fastest way to delete your account.

  1. Open Printy 3D and sign in with Google or Apple.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap My Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Read the confirmation message.
  6. Tap Delete Account again to confirm.
  7. Wait for processing to finish. You will be signed out when deletion completes.

In-app deletion starts immediately after you confirm.

Option B: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you do not need to reinstall it. You can request account deletion by email.

Email us at: printy31825@gmail.com

Please use the subject: Printy 3D Account Deletion Request

Include the following information:

  • The email address linked to your Printy 3D account.
  • Your Apple private relay email, if you signed in with Apple and used private email relay.
  • Your display name, if known.
  • A brief statement that you want your Printy 3D account and associated data deleted.

We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before processing the request.

Request account deletion

What data is deleted

When you delete your Printy 3D account, we delete or remove your profile, store, listings, uploaded media, saved preferences, notification tokens, and other account-linked data associated with your account, where applicable.

This may include:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account and Google or Apple sign-in link.
  • Your profile and account settings, including name, email address, profile photo, user ID, and preferences.
  • Your store profile and store data.
  • Your product listings, descriptions, and uploaded images or files.
  • Your likes, favorites, follows, and blocked-store lists.
  • Your product notification subscriptions.
  • Your push notification tokens and in-app notification records.
  • Your product share records tied to your account.
  • Files stored under your account in Firebase Storage.

What data may be retained

Some limited information may be retained in anonymized or de-identified form for legal obligations, security, fraud and abuse prevention, moderation, and support purposes.

Where retained, personal identifiers such as your name, email address, and profile photo are removed or replaced, and message content you sent may be cleared.

Examples of information that may be retained in anonymized, minimized, or de-identified form include:

  • Chat and conversation metadata: other participants may still see the conversation thread, but your account may appear as a deleted user and personal identifiers may be removed.
  • Support chat history: retained only where needed for operational or support integrity, with personal identifiers removed where applicable.
  • Abuse, report, and moderation records: retained where needed for safety, security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or platform moderation.
  • Aggregated analytics or crash logs: retained for service improvement, reliability, and security, and not linked to an identifiable account after deletion where possible.
  • Backups and third-party service records: residual copies may remain for a limited time according to the backup and retention practices of our service providers.

Processing time

In-app deletion is processed immediately after you confirm.

Email deletion requests are processed within 30 days of verified receipt.

Partial data deletion

Printy 3D currently provides full account deletion. We do not currently offer a separate web request flow for deleting only some data while keeping the account active.

Some user-created content may be removable inside the app before deleting your account, where the app provides that option.

Important notes

  • Printy 3D supports account sign-in through Google and Apple using Firebase Authentication.
  • Admin or internal accounts may require manual handling. If you believe this applies to you, contact us by email.
  • Printy 3D uses third-party infrastructure services such as Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Firebase Storage, Cloud Functions, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and related Google/Firebase services.
  • Deletion or anonymization applies to data stored in our app systems. Third-party providers may retain limited backup or operational records according to their own policies and legal requirements.
